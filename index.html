Bananas

By Omar Tounsi

Bananas are the best fruit. They are sweet and easy to eat. They are yellow and taste good. There are many things you can make with a banana. You can add them to your toast. You can make bread out of them. You can make pancakes out of them. You can add them to your cereal. You can add them to your oatmeal. In fact, they are the most sold fruit in the USA. It feels good that I share the same taste in fruit as the majority of the US population, which makes me love bananas even more.
